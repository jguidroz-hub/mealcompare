# Chrome Web Store Listing — Eddy

## Name
Eddy — Save on Food Delivery

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Compare food delivery prices across DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub & direct ordering. See the cheapest option instantly. Save $5-15/order.

## Detailed Description
Stop overpaying for food delivery! Eddy automatically detects your cart on DoorDash, Uber Eats, and Grubhub, then shows you the cheapest way to get that exact order — including direct ordering from the restaurant.

**How it works:**
1. Browse any food delivery app as usual
2. Add items to your cart
3. Click the Eddy icon to see price comparisons
4. Switch to the cheapest option with one click

**What Eddy shows you:**
• Real prices from Uber Eats (verified via API)
• Estimated prices from DoorDash & Grubhub
• Direct ordering links (Toast, Square, restaurant websites) — often 15-30% cheaper
• Full fee breakdown: subtotal, service fee, delivery fee, platform markup, tax
• Your potential savings on every order

**Why direct ordering saves you money:**
Delivery apps charge restaurants 15-30% commission, which gets passed to you as menu markups. When you order directly from a restaurant's website (Toast, Square, etc.), you skip those markups AND often get lower delivery fees.

**Currently supported metros:**
• Washington, DC (100+ restaurants with direct ordering links)
• Austin, TX (80+ restaurants with direct ordering links)

**Privacy-first:**
• No account required
• No data collection
• Cart data stays on your device
• Only communicates with our comparison API

**Free to use. No ads. No tracking.**
