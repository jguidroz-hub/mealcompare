## Personalization Variables
- [NAME]: Restaurant owner/manager name or "team"
- [RESTAURANT]: Full restaurant name
- [METRO]: URL slug (austin, dc, nyc, la, chicago)
- [SLUG]: Restaurant URL slug (e.g., terry-blacks-bbq)
- [CITY]: Display name (Austin, Washington DC, New York, LA, Chicago)
